About Me

I am a UX and UI Designer that began my career as a webpage/game designer and eventually became a frontend web developer, specializing in HTML and CSS. I found that visual design complements frontend development by approaching problems from a different creative angle, and frontend development helps focus all that creative energy into making a feasible product. UX/UI design has a similar balance of practicality and the atheistic. I am especially intrigued by the research process behind it all

From quantitative user research, to wire-framing and prototype testing and all the other building blocks used to create an effectual and intuitive experience for the user.
